How to Cultivate Healthy Eating Habits

Healthy eating is not just about losing weight or following a strict diet. It is about feeling great, having more energy, and stabilizing your mood. ___36

First, focus on how you feel after eating.1 ___37 This will help you foster healthy new habits and tastes. If you eat junk food, you might feel sluggish or tired. If you eat fresh, whole foods, you'll likely feel more energized.

Second, eat with others whenever possible. ___38 Sharing a meal with others has huge emotional benefits.2 It is a great way to relieve stress.

Third, listen to your body.3 ___39 Stop eating before you feel uncomfortably full. Many of us don't pay attention to these signals and keep eating until we are stuffed.

Finally, be patient with yourself. Changing your eating habits is a process.4 ___40 Don't get discouraged if you slip up once in a while. Focus on your long-term goals instead of short-term perfection.

📋 七选五 · 拖拽选项到正文空位
A The healthier the food, the better you will feel.
B Eating in a social setting allows you to model healthy eating habits.
C It takes time for your body to adjust to new eating habits.
D Ask yourself how you feel after eating certain foods.
E You should always consult a doctor before changing your diet.
F Pay attention to how your hunger cues feel.
G Healthy eating can be simple and enjoyable.
1
词汇衔接线索："focus on how you feel" 直接对应选项 D 中的 "Ask yourself how you feel"。这是典型的原词复现（feel 重复出现），且逻辑上构成"总—分"关系：先给出建议（focus on...），再具体化操作（ask yourself...）。
2
照应分析："Sharing a meal with others" 与前文 "eat with others" 构成词汇衔接（同义替换）。emotional benefits 是 relieve stress 的上位概念。注意：该句既承上又启下，是段落的核心论据。
3
连接词信号："listen to your body" 是本段主旨，后文 "signals" 和 "stuffed" 都属于身体信号的范畴。选项 F "Pay attention to how your hunger cues feel" 中的 hunger cues（饥饿信号）与 signals 构成同义替换关系。
4
逻辑链条分析："process" 一词是关键锚点。空后说 "Don't get discouraged"（不要气馁），暗示这是一个需要时间的过程。选项 C "It takes time for your body to adjust" 中的 "takes time" 与 "process" 完美呼应，且与 "be patient" 形成因果关系：因为需要时间适应（C），所以要有耐心（be patient）。
阅读实战 · B 篇
A Retired Teacher's Wildlife Sanctuary

John, a retired teacher, has spent the last decade transforming his backyard into a sanctuary for local wildlife.1 What started as a small project to grow some vegetables has blossomed into a thriving ecosystem.

In an effort to attract more pollinators, John began planting native flowers such as milkweed and coneflowers.2 He spent hours researching which plants would best support the local bee and butterfly populations. It wasn't just about the plants, though; he also installed several bird baths and a small pond.

According to local environmental experts, John's garden has become an important stopover for migratory birds.3 The variety of food and water sources he provides allows these birds to rest and refuel during their long journeys. John feels a great sense of accomplishment knowing that his efforts are making a tangible difference.

Neighbors who were initially skeptical about his "wild" garden have started to take notice.4 Many are now asking for advice on how they can create their own small-scale habitats. John is always happy to share his knowledge, hoping that his small backyard can inspire a larger movement for urban conservation.

📋 阅读理解 · 选择题

24. What was John's original intention for his backyard?

A To build a wildlife sanctuary.
B To grow some vegetables.
C To attract migratory birds.
D To inspire his neighbors.

25. Why did John plant milkweed and coneflowers?

A To attract pollinators.
B To beautify his garden.
C To grow food for his family.
D To sell at the local market.

26. What does the word "stopover" in paragraph 3 most probably mean?

A A permanent home.
B A temporary resting place.
C A feeding station.
D A breeding ground.

27. What can be inferred about John's neighbors?

A They have always supported John's project.
B They still think the garden is too wild.
C They have changed their attitude toward John's garden.
D They have built their own wildlife sanctuaries.
1
主旨定位：首句即主旨句——John 是退休教师，花了十年把后院变成野生动物庇护所。注意 "What started as... has blossomed into..." 这个对比结构，暗示他最初的目的（种菜）和最终的结果（生态系统）是不同的。这直接对应第 24 题。
2
细节题定位句："In an effort to attract more pollinators"（为了吸引更多传粉者）直接回答第 25 题。milkweed（马利筋）和 coneflowers（锥花）都是蜜源植物，专门为蜜蜂和蝴蝶种植。注意 "In an effort to" 这个目的状语结构，是高考常考的细节定位信号。
3
词义猜测："stopover" 后文紧跟 "rest and refuel during their long journeys"（在长途旅行中休息和补给）。这是典型的上下文释义——"休息+补给+旅途中" 共同指向"临时歇脚点"的含义，对应 B 选项 "A temporary resting place"。注意 migratory birds（候鸟）这个关键词也暗示了"迁徙途中的临时停留"。
4
推断题解析："initially skeptical"（起初持怀疑态度）与 "have started to take notice"（开始关注）形成鲜明对比。这组对比结构说明邻居的态度发生了由怀疑到认可的转变。关键词 "initially" 暗示后来有所改变，直接对应 C 选项 "changed their attitude"。A 选项错在 "always"（一直），与 "initially skeptical" 矛盾。
